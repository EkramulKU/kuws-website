Beyond the Horizon

By Farhana Akter October 26, 2025

The world of words is ever-changing, and yet, its essence remains rooted in the same desire — to connect minds and hearts across time and space. At Khulna University, young writers are breaking beyond traditional boundaries, redefining what it means to be a storyteller in the digital age.

“Beyond the Horizon” explores this evolution of literary expression among students who find power not only in writing but in reflection. From short stories and poems to campus journalism, each form carries the pulse of a generation seeking purpose, clarity, and truth.

The Writers’ Society, in many ways, serves as the bridge between passion and publication. With workshops, peer mentorship, and collaborative critique, it offers a rare platform for emerging voices to flourish — nurturing not just skill, but a sense of belonging.

As digital platforms amplify young voices, the challenge now lies not in reaching an audience, but in resonating with them. And in this challenge, perhaps, lies the true beauty of writing — the endless pursuit of meaning beyond the visible horizon.
