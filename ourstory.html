Editorial Quiz- 2025

This competition is specially designed for university students who wish to go beyond the surface-level news and delve into the depth and significance of events. By reading the editorials of the country's leading daily newspapers, you will not only gain insights into national and global affairs but also get a chance to sharpen your analytical skills. Participants will be required to regularly read the editorials published in two major national dailies of Bangladesh—Prothom Alo and Samakal—from September 20 to September 30, 2025. The quiz questions will be based on the content, author's perspective, and arguments presented in the editorials from this specific period.
